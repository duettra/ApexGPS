# ApexGPS

A free Android hiking app. Open offline maps, import GPX tracks, mark waypoints, and share your live location with anyone.

Currently in **closed beta**. Public release on the Play Store coming soon.

## What lives at this domain

- [`/loc?lat=…&lon=…`](/loc?lat=25.165183&lon=55.409717) — landing page for shared locations. Opens directly in ApexGPS on devices that have it installed; shows a web map preview otherwise.
- [`/privacy.html`](/privacy.html) — privacy policy.

## Source

The public site source (this repo) is at [github.com/duettra/ApexGPS](https://github.com/duettra/ApexGPS).
