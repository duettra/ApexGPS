# ApexGPS

A free Android hiking app. Open offline maps, import GPX tracks, mark waypoints, and share your live location with anyone.

- [**Getting started**](/docs/getting-started/) — first launch, permissions, a five-minute walkthrough.
- [**The map screen**](/docs/map/) — gestures, compass, follow-me, every control explained.
- [**Tracks**](/docs/tracks/) · [**Waypoints**](/docs/waypoints/) — import, edit, filter, sort, delete.
- [**Share & navigate**](/docs/share-and-navigate/) — send your location, receive shared spots, navigate to a target.
- [**Offline maps**](/docs/offline-maps/) — download regions for use without signal.
- [**Backup & restore**](/docs/backup/) — save and migrate your data.
- [**Settings**](/docs/settings/) — every option.
- [**FAQ & troubleshooting**](/docs/faq/)
