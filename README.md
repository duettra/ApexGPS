## What's inside

|  |  |  |
|---|---|---|
| 🗺️ **Six map providers** <br> OpenTopoMap, OSM, ESRI Topo & Relief, Satellite, Outdoors | 📥 **Offline regions** <br> Download tiles by area before you head out | 📍 **GPX import & export** <br> Open standard — bring tracks from anywhere |
| ⛰️ **Elevation profiles** <br> From your recording or fetched from terrain DEM | 🛰️ **Live GPS recording** <br> Foreground tracking, survives screen-off & reboots | ✏️ **Track planner** <br> Sketch a route by tapping the map; save as a real track |
| ☁️ **Hiking-grade weather** <br> Per-waypoint hourly forecasts, no API key | 🧭 **Compass & navigation** <br> Bearing & distance to any waypoint | 💾 **Backup & restore** <br> One-tap ZIP of every track, waypoint, region |

---

## Why ApexGPS?

- **No subscription** — every feature, free, forever.
- **No tracking** — your location stays on your device. We don't run servers.
- **Open formats** — GPX in, GPX out. Your data stays portable.
- **Six languages** — English · Deutsch · Français · Español · Polski · العربية.
- **Lean** — < 4 MB APK, runs on Android 8+.
- **Honest** — closed-testing alpha while it earns its production launch.
